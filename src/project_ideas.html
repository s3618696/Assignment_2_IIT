Project Ideas

Overview

The project will be an AI mobile app for growing food at home. This app acts like a farmer who will help the users choose the most suitable plants, provide them with instructions on how to take care of their plants step-by-step, and push notifications for reminding users to keep an eye on their plants.


Motivation

As we all know, the world is suffering from the Corona Virus. Growing fresh vegetables at home are met by people around the world during the execution of a social blockade or social dilation to prevent the translation of the Covid-19 disease. According to Quang Nam Online Newspaper, "The consumption of seeds is increased globally. In Russia, the demand for seed increased by 20%-30% in March compared to the same period last year." To support the need for clean vegetable farming, I came up with this idea of doing this project to help people in productive plants, effectively, through which they would increase interest in agriculture, improve health, and protect the environment.


Description

Step 1: Preparation

Users can search for a plant or take a picture of the plant they want to grow. The app will help detect which type of the plant is, and output to the user plant name, plant description, as well as the best environment to grow the plant.

After users allow the app to access their location, the app will give the user advice on whether they should grow the vegetable depending on the location geography and weather.

Step 2: Growing and Harvesting

Based on the instructions, users will start growing their own plants and take pictures of the plants weekly, then submit them to the app. The apps will analyse the status of the plants according to the size, the colour, the shape of leaves, … and present a detailed planting roadmap for users on how to keep nurturing their plants in one week. For example:
• Day 1:
300 ml water (optimal time range: 6AM-7AM)
30g sodium
• Day 2:
300 ml water in the morning (optimal time range: 6AM-7AM)
Ensure that the surrounding temperature is 260C
• Day 3:
300 ml water in the morning (optimal time range: 6AM-7AM)
20g nitrogen fertilizer
• Day 4:
300 ml water in the morning (optimal time range: 6AM-7AM)
10g phosphorous
• Day 5:
300 ml water in the morning (optimal time range: 6AM-7AM)
Ensure that the surrounding temperature is 260C
• Day 6:
300 ml water in the morning (optimal time range: 6AM-7AM)
15g potassium
• Day 7:
300 ml water in the morning (optimal time range: 6AM-7AM)
Ensure that the surrounding temperature is 260C

Notifications will also be sent to users based on this roadmap to remind them to take care of the plants. Throughout weeks, tips to grow the plant better will also be sent to users and updated to the roadmap.

Step 3: After Harvesting

When it is time for harvesting, the app will give users tips to recover the plants and provides a new roadmap to continue growing for the next harvesting.

Additional Features

This app will also have a social media platform called Home Grow Community to post their plant processes, share their experiences or even trade their products with each other.

After this app is launched and able to gain a large number of users, we will install a new feature which recommends users a specific type of fertilizer or necessary gardening tool when growing their plants. This will allow brands of fertilizer and gardening utensils to promote their products to a wider pool of potential customers and in return, we will charge these brands for advertising fees as a financial resource to maintain this application.


Technologies and Tools

This app development will some reliable computers that can handle the convolutional neuron network's training model along with sufficient kernel on the virtual machine.

Hardware requirements:
• GPU: RTX 2070 or RTX 2080 Ti of 8GB for better performance but does not cost too much
• CPU: Intel Core i9 i9-9900K because of the need of multithreading of this app (Petrova 2020)
• RAM of at least 8GB
• Power supply unit
• Cooling for CPU and GPU
• Monitors
(Dettmers 2018)
Software requirement
• Adobe Illustrator (to illustrate written instructions demonstrated on the interface)
• Dataset of types of different plants to use in model training
• Deep learning API: Keras (because of its simplicity and flexibility)


Skills Required

• Understand how to use Python, because Keras is a Python-based API
• Design skills
• Knowledge of statistics, calculus, linear algebra and probability (Machine Learning Crash Course 2019)
• Knowledge of data visualization (for explanatory analysis) (Elite Data Science n.d.)
• Understand how to apply the best algorithm when training the model (Elite Data Science n.d.)


Outcome

• This app will benefit those who enjoy planting and want to spend their leisure time on planting activities, especially in the context of pandemic nowadays
• Provide cleaner and more nutritious vegetables to users
• Reduce the usage of pesticides and other detrimental chemicals, which can save the environment as well
